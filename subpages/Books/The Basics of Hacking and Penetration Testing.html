The Basics of Hacking and Penetration Testing

Summary

The Basics of Hacking and Penetration Testing is a book by Patrick Engebretson that aims to introduce the reader to hacking and penetration testing. The book focuses on teaching a reader what basic steps and tools will be necessary to complete a hack or penetration test. This book is important because it is more approachable than other hacking introductions. This book is more targeted for beginners, and it makes an effort to keep the reader from drowning in new information. Despite this, the book still contains the broad picture of how multiple different hacking techniques and tools link together.
