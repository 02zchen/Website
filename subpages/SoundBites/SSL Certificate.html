SSL Certificate

Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) is a global standard security technology that is used to encrypt communication between two devices, typically your personal device and a company’s server. In order to establish this encrypted connection, the companies must install an SSL certificate on their server. There are multiple ways to tell if their website has an SSL Certificate: if the web address starts with https, if there is a padlock symbol next to the address, and if there is digital certificate image somewhere in the website. Now why is an SSL Certificate important? This certificate is extremely important if you are submitting any sort of personal information to the website. Without the certificate encrypting your data, anyone can read the internet traffic between you and the website’s server. This means that they can steal your credit card information, take your passwords, etc.The SSL Certificate also ensures that you are indeed visiting the site that your intended to visit, and not a bogus, phising site.All in all, it is important to verify that a website’s server has an SSL Certificate before entering confidential information in order to protect your information from being stolen from anybody with malicious intents.
