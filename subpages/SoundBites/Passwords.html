Passwords

Passwords are the most well known way of securing your accounts andinformation online, but do you know your passwords might not be up topar? There are many ways to ensure that your passwords are hard to crackinto but there are three rules I always keep in my head. Make themcomplex, inconsistent, and inconspicuous. The first rule refers to makingsure you aren’t having a password with only lowercase letters, make it longwith symbols, numbers, and uppercase letters. The second rule means youdon’t want to have your passwords be the same on each account, oncesomeone gets into one of your accounts they can get into all of them if you keep the same password. Lastly, the third rule means don’t make it obviouswhat it might be, first and last name, username, birthday putting any of thatin your password makes it much easier to crack into your accounts.Following these three rules will make your accounts much harder to getinto, and your information much safer.
